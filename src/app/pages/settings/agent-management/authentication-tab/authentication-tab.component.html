Two Factor Authentication (2FA)

Significantly improve the security of your district account and keep the information safe by enabling 2FA.

What Is Two Factor Authentication?

Two factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring not only your username and password but also a unique code sent to your email before you can log in, this means that even if someone knows your password, they cannot access your account without the additional verification code.

How Is Two Factor Authentication Helpful?

  1. Enhanced Security: Protects against unauthorized access as users can only login if they have access to their email, where a unique code will be sent.
  2. Peace of Mind: Ensures only authorized users can access their accounts from both the web and the mobile admin app.
  3. Anti-Phishing: Reduces the phishing attacks.

What Happens When Two Factor Authentication Is Turned On?

  1. Login Process: After entering the username and password, users will receive a unique code on their email.
  2. Email Notifications: Every login attempt will be paired with an email notification containing the code.
  3. Verification: Enter the code in provided field to complete the login process.
  4. For enhanced security, all mobile app users will have to re-authorize once 2FA is enabled.
